About

Thincr Technologies India Pvt Ltd is founded in 2016 with the objective to innovate technology based product in the sector of healthcare, education and consumer products. Our tag-line is “Think Innovate & Create". We want to focus our efforts for developing 3D printers and compositions for pharmaceutical application. We aimed to be pioneer in India to have patented and cost effective 3D-printed dosage form with indigenous technology having commercialization potential.

Since inception we have delicately worked lot on our 3D printing project “3D-Thincr”. Our objective in this project is to enhance the reach-ability of 3D-printing technology and its benefit to common people. We have developed in-depth knowledge about 3D-Printer technology. we have continued eye on happenings in the field of application of 3D-printing technology in pharmaceutical and medical application. We believe that 3DPrinting will continue to revolutionize the development of new generations of pharmaceutical formulations that are safe and effective.

We have developed capabilities of manufacturing our own FDM type 3D-printer.Having strong pharmaceutical background and the understanding developed in manufacturing & working of 3D-printers, we foresee a immense scope for integrating our knowledge base to develop 3D printers usable in pharmaceutical development. We are working on development of novel 3D printed pharmaceutical formulations.
